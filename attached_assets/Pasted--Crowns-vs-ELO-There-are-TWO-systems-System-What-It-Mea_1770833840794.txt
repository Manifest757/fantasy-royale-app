3️⃣ Weekly Streak System
If you play at least once every week:
Your streak grows

4 weeks


8 weeks

 Streak resets.

It builds routine.

4️⃣ Monthly Giveaway Engine
Every month:
Your total Crowns = your giveaway entries


More Crowns = more chances


But you must have played at least once that month (anti-cheat protection)


Important:
Crowns are NOT spent


They are just used as entry weight


So someone with 5,000 Crowns has 5,000 entries.
 Someone with 200 Crowns has 200 entries.
It rewards long-term loyalty.

5️⃣ Contest System (Independent)
Each contest:
Has its own scoring rules


Has its own leaderboard


Has tie-breakers


Has its own winner


Winning a contest:
Gives Crown rewards


May give badges


Does NOT directly change overall leaderboard rank


Contests are isolated games.
